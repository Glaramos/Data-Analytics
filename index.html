    Human Resources

    In this project an analysis has been carried out in the Human Resources Area, a very important area in companies.



    Description

    In this project an analysis has been carried out in the Human Resources Area, a very important area in companies. The amount of active personnel has been analyzed in the different areas of the company (fictional), the age of the person, the average salary obtained in each respective area; the year of contracting and performance of each employee. The analysis of the data obtained describes that there are 207 active employees, of which 7 employees are between 21-25 years old and 11 employees are 51-55 years old. The average for each area such as IT, Administrative, Sales, Production is $6729. The employees who stand out in their unemployment are 162 employees. The year in which the most employees were hired was between 2016-2018.
